COVID-19 and Agriculture Research in Rwanda.

The project is a research project that is set up to study COVID-19 impact on Agriculture in Rwanda. Agriculture is the main stay of some of the local communities and now with COVID-19, there could be greater impact that negatively affects these communities. The research hopes to study the impact and relationship between these two.
